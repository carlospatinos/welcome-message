What I expect of you in this course
  • I expect you to log in every weekday. Come to your online course at least as often as you come to your face-to-face classes.
  • I expect you to effectively manage your time. Please note: there are due dates for the assignments in this course, and there are penalties for late assignments. Read the Course Info page for details, and of course contact me with questions.
  • I expect you to complete your own work and to contribute to the class discussions. You should be familiar with Michigan Virtual’s policies on academic integrity and follow them.
  • I expect you to follow the information outlined in the Advanced Placement Course Policy document.
  • I expect you to read the class Announcements every day.
  • I expect you to check your course messages every day.
  • I expect you to be respectful and considerate when communicating and working with classmates

Please contact me when you have questions or if you’re feeling frustrated with class. I am here to help!
