What you can do to help your students
  • Take a look at the pacing guide under the Course Info section and compare it to your student's progress.
  • Review your student's results on graded assignments and encourage them to reach out for help when they struggle.
  • Encourage your student to use the additional resources suggested in many of the lessons. There are links to videos and tutorials and so forth that are quite useful.
  • Keep an eye on the weekly announcements I post. These always include a reminder of the pacing a student should be following.
  • Encourage your student to use the Discussion Board to get in touch with other students, to get help from a peer, and generally to hang out with classmates.
