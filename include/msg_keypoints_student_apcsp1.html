Key points for students

This is an AP course and that means certain expectations:

  • There are weekly due dates for assignments, with penalties for late assignments.
  • Most assignments can be resubmitted for full credit up to the due date
  • Exams are password-protected. I will email mentors the passwords about a month into the class.
  • All exams must be proctored.
  • Assessments (quizzes, tests, exams) cannot be retaken
  • With my approval most other assignments can be resubmitted for full credit.

Details for policies can be found in the Course Info section. Please contact me anytime you have questions.
